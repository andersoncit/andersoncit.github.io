Grooming

We’ve talked a lot about clothing items. I want to emphasize the cherry on top which is your grooming state. You must have a nice hairdo and if you have facial hair, it must be trimmed and look neat. Remember the key is looking respectable and classy at the same time. This will look better than going for a messy outside the box look.
