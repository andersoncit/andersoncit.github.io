Introduction

Anderties is a company that provides people everywhere with ties that are ahead of the times and offers them at an affordable price. The goal is that anyone can come to Anderties to “’up their fashion game” and feel confident by dressing sharp. The difference maker with shopping at Anderties vs any other tie store, is that Anderties doesn’t only offer styles that are popular at the moment, but we make sure that you are being a trendsetter by wearing our newest design ties. Anderties sets the bar at a new level that is impossible to replicate. Wearing Anderties will set you apart from the crowd and give you a new sense of self that you have been searching for. Anderties was founded by Aidan Anderson while he was in college. Aidan decided to sell some unique ties he owned and saw a lot of success in doing so. It was at that moment that he saw a vision for what ties could be.
